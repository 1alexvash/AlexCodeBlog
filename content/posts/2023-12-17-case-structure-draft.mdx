Good case checklist

1. case's about solution rather than your excellent work
2. fall in love with the formula: "We initially thought, then happened, to fix it, and finally"
3. Focus on one subject
4. Do not hint that the client is an idiot
5. Add charts and diagrams explaining the problem and solution. Shows before and after.
6. Conclusion work with us

***

Structure:

1. Title: simple name
2. Situation and problem
3. Implementation
4. Solution
5. Conclusion
